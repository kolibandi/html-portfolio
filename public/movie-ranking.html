The Best Movies According to Ilker

My top 3 movies off all-time.

    The Dark Knight

    The Dark Knight showcases the breathtaking battle between the Joker, who dances in the darkness of Gotham, and the caped crusader Batman!

    Fight Club

    Fight Club vividly portrays the psychological battle between Tyler Durden and an unnamed narrator.

    Forrest Gump

    The movie that takes me back to my childhood. Forrest Gump, portrayed by Tom Hanks, follows the extraordinary journey of a simple-minded man through pivotal moments in American history.
